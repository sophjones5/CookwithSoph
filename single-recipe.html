Steak and Chips

Steak and chips is truly a classic meal. I make this usually atleast twice a week. There is definately methods and tips which can elevate this meal. Nothing beats tender well cooked high quality steak with crispy salty fluffy chips. Also of course these chips are fried in beef tallow no inflammatory seed oils used here.

prep time

15 minutes

cook time

30 minutes

servings

2 servings

Tags: Beef Potatoes Dinner

instructions

Step 1

Wash then slice to potatoes into thin fry shapes.

Step 2

Heat beef tallow in small saucepan until it reaches 325 degrees, turn down the heat and try and keep tempreture around this mark.

Step 3

Add the chips in batches and fry until golden. Transfer to a sheet pan to keep warm in the oven on 160 degrees.

Step 4

When the last batch of fries is going in add olive oil to a frying pan. Season and then cook steaks for 3-4 minutes on each side - only flipping once.

Step 5

Salt the chips and serve.

ingredients

2 Steaks

4 Potatoes

Beef Tallow

Salt

Pepper

Olive Oil

tools

Small Saucepan

Frying Pan

Sheet Pan
